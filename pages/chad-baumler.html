Scott Moore, CFA

Scott Moore is the President and Co-Chief Investment Officer of Nuance and is a co-owner of the firm. He founded the firm in November, 2008 and is also the Lead Portfolio Manager for Nuance Concentrated Value and Nuance Mid Cap Value. Scott has more than 31 years of investment experience, more than 29 years of value investment analyst experience and more than 23 years of portfolio management experience using a classic value approach.
