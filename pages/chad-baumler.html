Scott Moore, CFA

Scott Moore is the President and Co-Chief Investment Officer of Nuance and is a co-owner of the firm. He founded the firm in November, 2008 and is also the Lead Portfolio Manager for Nuance Concentrated Value and Nuance Mid Cap Value. Scott has more than 31 years of investment experience, more than 29 years of value investment analyst experience and more than 23 years of portfolio management experience using a classic value approach.

For the decade before co-founding Nuance, Scott managed more than $10 billion in institutional, intermediary and mutual fund assets for American Century Investments (ACI). Prior to becoming a Portfolio Manager, he spent three years as an Investment Analyst at ACI, specializing in the telecommunications, utility and industrial sectors. He also worked as an Investment Analyst at Boatmen's Trust Company in St. Louis, Missouri, and at ACI as a Fixed Income Investment Analyst.

Scott holds a Bachelor of Science (BS) in finance from Southern Illinois University, and a Master of Business Administration (MBA) with an emphasis in finance from the University of Missouri. He is a CFA
